Guidebook for Prefects - Rules And Regulations

Prohibited Items

Rules

Be serious and proactive. Give warnings and record names if students do not heed warnings. Expel students who are outside the classroom and send them back to their own class. Record names of students with prohibited items (e.g., scissors, correction tape, cutter) and pass to consuls after your shift. Consuls will then bring students and items to the discipline teacher. Ensure students are quiet and reading their books in the morning. No sleeping or chatting. Record names of non-compliant students and pass to consuls after your shift. Reach your duty station on time. Repeated lateness will result in punishment. Give warnings to students sleeping or talking in class. Record names of those who do not comply and pass to consuls after your shift. Be polite and greet teachers; do not ignore them. When taking attendance from consuls, line up. After your shift, return to class in a single line; walking in groups is not allowed. Every Monday and Thursday, check students' nails and hair: Provide nail clippers to students with long nails and have them cut nails on the spot. Write down the names of students with long hair and pass to consuls after your shift.
